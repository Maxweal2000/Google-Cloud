Welcome to Dumfries and Galloway

Enjoy a stay in our Victorian home with unique rooms named after Scottish cities such as Glasgow, Edinburgh, Aberdeen, and Dundee. Perfect for adults looking to relax in the beautiful west of Scotland with self-service accommodations for added privacy.

Our house, built in the early 1900s, has a rich history with a touch of Victorian charm. Interested in a few ghost stories? Visit our About page for more insights into the history of our home.

Cocoabean Company

Bring your kids to Twynholm and enjoy a chocolate-making experience at the Cocoabean Company. Create sweet treats together!

Mountain Biking

Explore Dalbeattie woods or Gorsebank by bike. Find trails perfect for hiking and biking.

Sandyhills Beach

Visit Sandyhills Beach just outside Dalbeattie for beautiful views and a peaceful escape towards Kippford.

Our Gardens

Discover beautiful garden spots like Threave Gardens in Castle Douglas. A must-see for nature lovers!
